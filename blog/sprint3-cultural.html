Core: EQ and IQ

What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is:

  • Being conscious of the present moment
  • Tuning in with body whether it be breath or sensation.
  • Understanding others emotions and being about to what things are yours and what things are theirs
  • Knowing oneself and being understanding to self
  • Noticing feelings and thoughts as they come up, greeting them with curiousity instead of reacting to them
  • Changing negative thought patterns into useful positive ones

Why is it important?

Both EQ and IQ are important in understanding and learning in life. I think EQ is important in more of a holistic way, it creates understanding, acceptance among differences, connection with others and connects to a deep part of a human's needs like being seen, heard and understood to name a few. It makes it easier to manage and interact with other humans.
In a job context being mindful and having EQ is a desirable trait as it is more conducive for teamwork and ease and understanding in the workplace. This means someone that has a higher EQ makes them more hireable.
